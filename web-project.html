Our Project Journey

Welcome to the official website of Team 39, The Handlers! This website serves as a showcase of our collaborative project for the SITE 1101 Principles of Information Systems course. Here's an overview of what this project is all about:

Innovative Web Design

Our primary goal was to design a functional, visually appealing website that represents the collective efforts of our team. From structuring pages to styling and adding interactivity, this project showcases the fundamentals of web development and teamwork.

Hour of Code Participation

As part of our project, we actively participated in the global *Hour of Code* initiative, promoting the importance of coding and computational thinking. This section highlights our experience and the impact of this event on fostering creativity and problem-solving skills.

Collaboration and Learning

Throughout this project, we emphasized teamwork and skill development. Each member contributed uniquely, from creative design to technical implementation, ensuring that our final product meets the highest standards.
