The Hour of Code

On 6th of November, we, The Handlers, conducted Hour of Code event at Sumgait city School No.1 to introduce children to the computer science world. We gathered children from different schools and neighborhoods and decided to conduct Hour of Code event in School No.1 as it was provided with necessary computer equipment. Our goal was to share our knowledge with children to spark a light towards computer sciences, even it was a small one.

Progress

Through the session, students not only engaged but also deeply involved in solving loop challenges. Familiarity with Minecraft and game structure of the exercises caught their attention and kept them motivated to complete challenging tasks. Focus and determination to solve tasks were signal that showed interest and desire to learn coding concepts.

Conclusion

Overall, Hour of Code left students excited and curious about the world of coding. Many participants shared that they have liked the session, and they are interested in diving deeper into computer science. Their positive feedback encouraged us to continue organizing events like this to expand coding education. Based on the feedback, there are ways to make future events even better.
